About us

Our Goals

With recent supply shortages, the value of vehicles has gone through the roof. Our main goal is to help you capitalize on this opportunity.

We want to make the process of selling your car as painless and hassle-free as possible. We don't need you to fill out tons of forms or wait on hold forever to sell your car. The quicker we buy your car, the better!

Unlike a dealership, our goal isn't to get you to buy something else. We want there to be as little pressure as possible for you.

Who are we?

Car Truck Buyer is a brand new company made in partnership with the Holiday Auto Group in Whitesboro, Texas.

We noticed dealerships were struggling to keep inventory on their lots, and a lot of consumers who didn't need their vehicles anymore.

This is where the idea of Car Truck Buyer came along. We could facilitate the buying of vehicles without the consumer feeling like they were being pressured by a dealership.
